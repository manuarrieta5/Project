Logical-mathematical Intelligence

Logical-mathematical intelligence refers to the capacity to analyze problems logically, carry out mathematical operations, and investigate issues scientifically.

People with logical-mathematical intelligence, such as Albert Einstein and Bill Gates, have an ability to develop equations and proofs, make calculations, and solve abstract problems with great facility.

    Some potential careers could be:
  • Mathematician
  • Accountant
  • Statistician
  • Scientist
  • Computer Analyst
